Since he was a little boy, Elmer K. Waffleopalous IV had one dream: to build his own globally dominant, omnichannel waffle creation and distribution company. Only two things stood in his way: his inability to make waffles (or even have a basic understanding of their ingredients), and the market superiority of the local favorite, Waverly's Waffles (now a division of Wafflecorp).

With time, determination, and a substantial loan from his father, Waffleopalous and his battalion of attorneys used the power of their bold vision and relentless legal pressure to convince Waverly's owner Priscilla Waverly to part with her recipes and properties, both physical and intellectual. Bringing together the sweet, kindly approach of Waverly's Waffles (now a division of Wafflecorp) and marrying it with the ruthlessly efficient process of Wafflecorp has proven to be nothing short of miraculous (and tasty!) for our shareholders. The result is the emergence and complete market saturation of Wafflecorp. After all, Waffleopalous believes that which does not kill you, only makes you bigger and batter. And he made it come true with a real-life #rags2waffles success story!

Today, as the global leader in the research and development, manufacturing, and distribution of the most delicious waffles on the planet, Wafflecorp (formerly Waverly's Waffles) remains committed to bringing the highest quality waffle and waffle-adjacent products to its customers' plates. With more than 6,000 franchises, pop-up carts, and Waffle-Me-Now!™ (our automated Waffle-as-a-Service conveyor belts) in 140 countries on six continents, Wafflecorp consistently puts more waffles in more human bellies worldwide than any other worldwide waffle company concern. In 2021 alone, Wafflecorp produced more than 17,400,000 battered and beaten waffles from our main factory at Wafflecorp HQ (former location of Waverly's Waffles).
